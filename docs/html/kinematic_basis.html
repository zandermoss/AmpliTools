amplitools.kinematic_basis

class amplitools.kinematic_basis.Basis(spin, npoint, symmetric=False)

A minimal basis of Mandelstam invariants.

Provides methods for conversion of MRational and MRing objects to a minimal, on-shell basis of invariants. Currently handles scalars only.

eject_masses(r)

Eject masses from MRing generator pairs to polynomial ring.
